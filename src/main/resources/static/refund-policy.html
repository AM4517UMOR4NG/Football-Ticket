Detailed Refund Policy

Full Refund (100%)

Within 24 hours of booking

You can cancel your booking and receive a full refund within 24 hours of making the reservation, regardless of the event date.

Event cancellation by organizer

If the event is cancelled by the organizer, you will automatically receive a full refund including all fees.

Venue change or significant modification

If the venue changes or there are significant modifications to the event that affect your ability to attend.

Partial Refund (50-80%)

7-30 days before event

Cancellations made between 7 and 30 days before the event date are eligible for a partial refund.

  • 7-14 days: 80% refund
  • 15-21 days: 70% refund
  • 22-30 days: 50% refund

Processing fees

A processing fee of 5% will be deducted from all partial refunds to cover administrative costs.

No Refund

Within 7 days of event

No refunds are available for cancellations made within 7 days of the event date.

No-show

If you do not attend the event without prior cancellation, no refund will be provided.

Personal circumstances

Personal circumstances such as illness, travel issues, or other personal reasons are not eligible for refunds within the no-refund period.

How to Request a Refund

Online Process

  1. 1 Log into your FootballTix account
  2. 2 Go to "My Bookings" section
  3. 3 Select the booking you want to cancel
  4. 4 Click "Cancel Booking" and confirm
  5. 5 Refund will be processed automatically

Important Notes

  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method used for the booking.
  • Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.
  • You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed.
  • Service fees and taxes are non-refundable unless the event is cancelled by the organizer.

Exceptions and Special Cases

Force Majeure Events

In cases of force majeure events (natural disasters, government restrictions, etc.), we may offer special refund arrangements or credit for future events.

Note: Each case is evaluated individually, and decisions are made based on the specific circumstances.

VIP and Premium Tickets

VIP and premium tickets may have different refund policies. Please check the specific terms when purchasing these tickets.

Tip: Contact our VIP support team for premium ticket refunds.

Season Passes and Packages

Season passes and multi-event packages have specific refund policies. Please refer to the terms and conditions provided at the time of purchase.

Important: Partial usage may affect refund eligibility for packages.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

Yes, you can transfer your ticket to another person up to 24 hours before the event. This can be done through your account dashboard or by contacting our support team.

What if I lose my ticket?

If you lose your ticket, you can download it again from your account dashboard. For physical tickets, contact our support team for replacement options.

Do I get a refund if the event is postponed?

If an event is postponed, your ticket remains valid for the new date. If you cannot attend the new date, you may be eligible for a refund depending on the circumstances.

How long does it take to receive my refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days. However, the time it takes to appear in your account depends on your bank or payment provider.

Need Help with a Refund?

Our support team is here to help you with any refund-related questions or issues.
