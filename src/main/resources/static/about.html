Our Story

Founded in 2023, FootballTix was born from one simple idea: make football accessible to every fan. We partner with top leagues and clubs to provide verified tickets and a trusted booking experience.

Quick Facts

  • • Trusted by fans worldwide
  • • Secure payments & verified tickets
  • • Partnerships: Premier League, LaLiga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Champions League

Our Mission

To provide the best football ticketing experience worldwide, making it easy and secure for fans to get to their favorite matches.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for football fans globally — connecting millions of supporters to stadium experiences.
