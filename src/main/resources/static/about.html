Our Story

Founded in 2023, FootballTix was born from a simple idea: to make football accessible to every fan around the world. We believe that the beautiful game should be experienced live, in the stadium, surrounded by thousands of passionate supporters.

What started as a small team of football enthusiasts has grown into a comprehensive platform that serves millions of fans across the globe. We partner with the world's biggest leagues and clubs to provide secure, reliable, and affordable ticket booking.

Today, FootballTix is proud to be the trusted choice for football fans worldwide, offering tickets to matches in the Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1, and the UEFA Champions League.
