Important Information

Your guide to a safe, secure, and unforgettable matchday experience.

Virtual Tickets

All tickets booked through FootballTix are digital. Ensure your smartphone is fully charged before heading to the stadium. Screenshots of tickets are strongly discouraged as NFC validation may be required.

Stadium Entry

Gates typically open 2 hours before kick-off. Arrive early to avoid queues. Valid identification matching the name on the ticket might be requested by stadium security at the turnstiles.

Clear Bag Policy

For enhanced safety, most stadiums implement a strict bag policy. Bags larger than A4 size (21.0 x 29.7cm) are generally not permitted. Luggage storage facilities are rarely provided.

Matchday Checklist

Must Do

  • Download or add tickets to your digital wallet before arriving.
  • Check your specific entrance gate printed on the ticket.
  • Follow instructions from stewards and local authorities.

Forbidden

  • No flares, smoke bombs, or pyrotechnics of any kind.
  • Umbrellas, selfie sticks, and professional cameras are banned.
  • Entering the pitch is a criminal offense and will lead to arrest.
