Essentials Guide

Important Information

Everything you need to know about booking, stadium entry, and match day protocols to ensure you have the best experience possible.

01

Ticket Authenticity

All tickets on FootballTix are 100% verified. We use direct encrypted digital ticketing systems to prevent fraud and ensure your seat is guaranteed.

02

Stadium Entry

Arrive at least 90 minutes before kick-off. You must have your digital ticket and a valid government ID. Screenshots of tickets are typically not accepted at the gates.

03

Safety Protocols

Fan safety is our priority. Be aware of the bag policy (restricted sizes) and prohibited items lists. Security checks are mandatory for all attendees.

Match Day Checklist

DO's

  • Charge your phone fully to display digital tickets.
  • Check your stadium gate number in advance.
  • Follow the directions of stadium stewards.

DON'Ts

  • Don't bring large backpacks (A4 size restriction usually applies).
  • Avoid bringing professional cameras without accreditation.
  • No pyrotechnics or dangerous objects.
