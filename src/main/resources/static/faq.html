Ticket Booking

To book tickets, simply browse our available matches, select your preferred seats, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or sign in to complete your booking. We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets.

Yes, you can modify or cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the match. Cancellations are subject to our refund policy. You can manage your bookings through your account dashboard or contact our customer support team.

Tickets are typically available for booking 3-6 months before the match date. For high-profile matches, tickets may be released earlier. We recommend booking as soon as possible to secure the best seats.

Payment & Pricing

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, and various digital wallets including Apple Pay and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

We charge a small service fee of 2.5% on all bookings to cover processing costs and platform maintenance. This fee is clearly displayed before you complete your purchase.

Yes, we offer full refunds for cancellations made more than 24 hours before the match. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a 50% refund. No refunds are available for no-shows.

Security & Safety

Absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect all payment information. We never store your credit card details on our servers. All transactions are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment gateways.

All tickets purchased through FootballTix are guaranteed authentic. We work directly with official ticket distributors and clubs. Each ticket comes with a unique QR code and security features to prevent fraud.

If a match is cancelled, we will automatically process a full refund to your original payment method within 5-7 business days. You'll also receive an email notification with details about the cancellation.

Customer Support

You can reach our customer support team through multiple channels: Live chat (available 24/7), email at support@footballtix.com, or phone at +1-800-FOOTBALL. We typically respond within 2 hours during business hours.

Our customer support is available 24/7 through live chat and email. Phone support is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST. For urgent match-day issues, we have dedicated emergency support available.

Yes! We have on-site support staff at major stadiums. Look for our FootballTix representatives wearing blue jackets with our logo. They can help with ticket issues, directions, and general assistance.
